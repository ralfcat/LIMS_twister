BloodAlert Donor Terms and Conditions

I acknowledge and agree to the following terms and conditions:

  1. Eligibility Confirmation

    2. I confirm that I meet the eligibility criteria for blood donation, including but not limited to age, weight, health, and medical history, as outlined by the blood donation center or relevant health authority.

  2. Accuracy of Information

    3. I certify that all personal information, medical history, and answers provided during the screening process are accurate and truthful to the best of my knowledge.

  3. Health and Safety

    4. I understand that blood donation involves potential risks, including but not limited to discomfort, bruising, dizziness, and in rare cases, more serious complications. I have been informed of these risks and voluntarily consent to proceed.

  4. Screening and Testing

    5. I understand that my blood will be tested for infectious diseases, including but not limited to HIV, hepatitis, and other blood-borne pathogens. I consent to these tests and understand that if my blood is found to be unsuitable for donation, it will not be used.

  5. Confidentiality

    6. I understand that my personal and medical information will be kept confidential and only shared with authorized personnel involved in the donation process, in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

  6. Voluntary Donation

    7. I acknowledge that this donation is voluntary and that I will not receive any payment or other incentives in exchange for my donation.

  7. Right to Refuse

    8. I understand that the blood donation center reserves the right to refuse my donation if it is determined that my blood does not meet safety standards or if there are concerns about my health.

  8. Post-Donation Care

    9. I agree to follow the post-donation care instructions provided by the blood donation center to ensure my safety and well-being after the procedure.

  9. Withdrawal of Consent

    10. I understand that I may withdraw my consent at any time before the blood collection begins, and doing so will not affect my right to receive medical care.

  10. Legal Release

    11. I release the blood donation center, its staff, and affiliated organizations from any liability, claims, or damages that may arise from my participation in the blood donation process, except in cases of negligence or willful misconduct.

  11. Data Privacy and Confidentiality

    12. I agree to the collection, storage, and processing of my personal information, including health data, within the BloodAlert. I understand that this information will be shared with the connected blood bank for purposes related to my donation and follow-up. The app and blood bank are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of my data in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR or similar local regulations.

  12. Communication and Notifications

    13. I consent to receiving notifications via email regarding my blood donation schedule, eligibility reminders, and urgent requests for donations. I understand that I can opt-out of non-essential communications at any time via the app's settings.
