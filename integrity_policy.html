Integrity Policy

Integrity policy

BloodAlert processes a considerable amount of personal and sensitive data, focusing on health information. In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation regulations, BloodAlert is the data controller and is obligated to inform you about the collection and processing of your personal data. The legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent, in accordance with Article 6.1(a). If you have any questions regarding the processing of your personal data, you can contact bloodalert.twister@gmail.com.


Which type of information we collect:
  • Personal Information: Name, sex, region, and email address. This information allows the system to match donors with nearby blood banks and allows the system to send out notifications to donors when blood is needed.

  • Sensitive information: Blood type and donation history. Blood type is collected for identifying which donors to notify when a specific blood type is needed. Donation history is provided as a feature for the donors to log their most recent donations, which the system will use to calculate when they are next eligible to donate.

GDPR

BloodAlert processes a considerable amount of personal and sensitive data, focusing on health information. In accordance with GDPR regulations, BloodAlert is obligated to inform you about the collection and processing of your personal data.


The purpose of processing personal data is to:

*Contact blood donors and manage their records, including gathering necessary information for communication, notifications, and health-related reminders.


The legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent, in accordance with Article 6.1(a) of the GDPR.


This is how BloodAlert stores and processes your data:
  1. The gathered information has a purpose for the system, in accordance with Article 4.1 of the GDPR
  2. The collected data is private and secure, in accordance with Article 4.1 as well as Article 32 of the GDPR
  3. The data collected has transparency and the user has access to their own data, in accordance with Article 13 and 14.

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
  1. The right to access your personal data, as per Article 15 of the GDPR.
  2. The right to rectify or erase your personal data in accordance with Articles 16 and 17 of the GDPR.
  3. The right to restrict the processing of your personal data according to Article 18 of the GDPR.
  4. The right to object to the processing of your personal data as outlined in Article 21 of the GDPR.
  5. The right to data portability under Article 20 of the GDPR.
  6. The right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority as stated in Article 77 of the GDPR.
  7. The right to withdraw your consent at any time, as specified in Article 13.2(c) as well as Article 7 of the GDPR.

To withdraw your consent, please contact us at bloodalert.twister@gmail.com.
